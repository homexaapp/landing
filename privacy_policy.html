Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information HomeXa collects, how we use it, with whom we share it, how long we keep it, and how you can exercise your rights.

Short summary

We only collect the personal information you provide when creating an account (for example: name, email, and any other fields required to register). We do not collect additional personal information from you during normal app use.

What we collect

Account information you provide during sign-up, such as your name, email address, and any other required registration fields. You may optionally provide additional profile information; we only store what you explicitly submit.

How we use your information

  • To create, authenticate, and manage your account.
  • To provide, operate, and improve the app features tied to your account.
  • To communicate with you regarding account-related matters and support requests.

Sharing and disclosure

  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • We may share limited account data with trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (for example: authentication, hosting, backups). Those providers are contractually required to protect your information and use it only to perform services for us.
  • We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

Data retention

We retain account information for as long as your account is active and as otherwise necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

If you ask us to delete your account, we will remove your personal information from our active systems within a reasonable timeframe except where retention is required by law.

Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. We regularly review our security practices, but no security measure can guarantee absolute protection.

Your rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data; note that deletion may result in loss of access to account-based features.

To make any of the above requests, contact us at the address below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

Children

The app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete the information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy in the app and update the "last-updated" date at the top.

Legal note
